Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Melunga (梅伦加), published by Melunga E-Learning, Level 7, World Trust Tower, 50 Stanley Street, Central, Hong Kong, takes the protection of your data seriously. This policy explains simply what we collect and why — and above all, everything we do not do.

1. What we don't do

Unlike many language-learning apps, Melunga:

  • Does not require creating an account, email, or password to use the app
  • Has no public profile, no social network, no ranking between users
  • Displays no advertising and shares no data for advertising purposes
  • Does not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes

2. What we collect

Device identifier: on your first visit, a random identifier is generated and stored locally in your browser (localStorage). It contains no personal information and is used only to remember your progress and, where applicable, your paid subscription status.

Learning progress: your statistics (level reached, cards viewed, daily streak) are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to our servers, except for the information strictly necessary to verify your subscription.

Approximate location: we use IP-based geolocation (automatically provided by our host, Netlify) solely to display the price in the correct currency (euro or dollar). This information is not recorded or retained.

Payment information: if you subscribe, payment is processed entirely by our secure payment provider (Airwallex). We never receive or store your bank card details. We only keep your device identifier and your access expiry date.

Account (email and password): to let you recover your subscription on a new device, an account is created at your first payment, linked to the email you provide. Your password is encrypted (hashed) before storage: we can never read it in plain text, and no one at Melunga has access to it. This email is used only to sign in to your subscription, never for commercial or advertising purposes.

3. Speech synthesis and recognition

For audio pronunciation, the text on cards is sent to our text-to-speech provider to generate the audio. If you use the microphone feature to practice speaking, your voice is recognized directly by your browser or operating system (on your device); no audio recording of your voice is ever sent to our servers or kept by us.

4. Retention period

Data stored locally on your device (progress, identifier) remains there until you clear your browser's browsing data. Information related to a paid subscription (device identifier and expiry date) is kept for as long as necessary to manage your access, then deleted.

5. Your rights

You can delete all local Melunga data at any time by clearing your browser's browsing data for this site. For any question regarding your data, or to request deletion of information related to your subscription, contact us at the address below.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this page.

7. Contact

For any question regarding this privacy policy: contact@melunga.com
